What does to "hire" mean?

  • In a second stage, both candidates repeated the identification task for {{time}} seconds.

You will choose which of the two candidates to hire, before knowing their actual performance in the second stage. You will only know the performance of the candidates in the first stage, but only the performance in the second stage will define your actual payment.
