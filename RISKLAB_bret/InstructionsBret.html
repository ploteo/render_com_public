Instructions

Below you will see a matrix containing 32 boxes on the screen. Of these 32 boxes, 31 contain 1 UMS each and 1 box contains a bomb. You don't know where the bomb is located. You know that the bomb can be placed with the same probability in every box.

IYour task is to indicate the number of boxes you want to collect. For each box you collect you earn 1 UMS. If you don't collect the bomb, your profit will be equal to the collected boxes. If you pick up the box where the bomb is placed, it will explode and your profit will be nil.

Once you have selected the number of boxes you want to collect, press the 'Stop' button and then the 'Continue' button. You will find out if you have collected the bomb only at the end of the experiment after selecting the number of boxes you want to collect.

At the end of the experiment, the total amount of UMS you earned in this part will be converted into Euro at the following exchange rate:

1 UMS = {{Constants.exchange_rate}} Euro
