This phase of the experiment is composed of three different rounds. In each of them, you will be assigned an initial endowment of 100 tokens and will be required to take your investment decision according to the rules explained before.

The screen will be divided into two parts: firstly, you will be required to indicate the share of the initial endowment that you are willing to invest. Secondly, you will be asked to indicate the chosen investment option among the alternatives. The investment options will be the same as indicated before

Option Description
A 75% chance of gaining 2x and 25% chance gaining x
B 50% chance of gaining 4x and 50% chance of losing half the investment x
C 25% chance of gaining 10x and 75% chance of losing your investment x

Once you have made your choices, you can click . The following screen will show you the payoff obtained. You can then click on the button in order to proceed to next round of the phase. In this phase, the payoff for each round will be computed according to the following function:

Ï€ = share of initial endowment not invested + outcome of the investment options chosen

At the end of each round you will be communicated your payoff. Final outcome of this phase will be the payoff of one randomly selected round among the three played.
