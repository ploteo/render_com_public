Your country asks you to allocate an initial endowment towards three specific policies. Furthermore, as part of its taxation system the state also promises to match 5% of your donation towards a charity in that field.

For each stage a specific policy proposal will be presented and we ask you to consider how much you are willing to contribute towards its implementation.

Each stage consists of 5 rounds, at the end of each round you will be able to see how much the other citizens decided to contribute.

Your initial endowment will be 100 tokens, and it will be renewed at the beginning of each round. At the end of each stage you will receive your payoff.
