Welcome to our experiment!

In this simulation, you will play the role of a student living in a city with several student apartments. Your goal is to reach the university by choosing a means of transportation.

You will have three means of transportation to choose from, each with its own cost, utility and emissions. Your task is to choose, in each round, which means of transportation you want to use. The game will consist of multiple rounds. In each round, you will get an endowment of 30 points to pay for your chosen transportation method.

All information will be presented in the following form. Please note that the values might change during the game:
